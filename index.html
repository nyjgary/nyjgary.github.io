Welcome to my homepage! I am a management consultant with an MBA from The University of Chicago looking to transition into data science. I have been learning R, Python, machine learning and other data science concepts through online resources. Below is a collection of some data science projects I have completed that chart my learning journey and showcase some of the skills I have learnt.

Peeking into the world of P2P lending with Prosper data

An exploratory data analysis of Prosper's 2005-2014 loan-level data, this study seeks to explore the characteristics of typical borrowers, nature of loans originated, and growth trajectory of the nation's first peer-to-peer lending marketplace. This project is coded in R and employs heavy usage of ggplot2, as well as other R packages like maps, dplyr, and reshape2. Check it out!

Investigating Titanic passengers' survival rates

My initiation into the world of NumPy and Pandas, this is a simple analysis of Titanic passenger data, coded in IPython. Through graphical plots and summary statistics, this study explores variables affecting likelihood of surviving the tragedy, including age, gender as well as other less obvious factors. Check it out!
